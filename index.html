About Me

I study Psychology at the National University of Mexico.

I specialized in Experimental Analysis of Behaviour, Cognitive Science and Statistics.

I'm passionate about data and human behavior.

Machine Learning

I have done natural language processing models for the National Institute for Educative Evaluation and the Secretary for Public Education in Mexico. Some of the implementations include unsupervised clustering for the classification of linguistic styles and supervised models to help in the decision process for rises and positions of teachers on the public service.

Statistics

I have done data analysis for the National Baking and Stock Commission in Mexico, usually for anomaly detection of time series. As well as standard analysis for local companies like the comparison between sets of data and general analytics for Monitoring, Management, and Deployment.
